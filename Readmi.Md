## 📌 Overview

**KinKeeper** is a modern, user-friendly relationship management application designed to help you nurture and maintain meaningful connections with your friends and loved ones.

✨ Track interactions
🎯 Set relationship goals
📊 Stay socially organized

---

## 🚀 Features

✨ **Friend Profiles**
Create and manage detailed profiles for your friends and loved ones.

📅 **Timeline Tracking**
Keep track of your interactions and important moments over time.

📱 **Responsive Design**
Works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

📊 **Status Monitoring**
Track relationship health and engagement.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

```bash
Frontend: React + Next.js
Styling: Tailwind CSS + DaisyUI
```
